KOKAN TOURISM

WHAT IS KOKAN ?

    Konkan is a beautiful coastal region of Maharashtra, nestled between the Arabian Sea and the Sahyadri Hills. Known for its pristine beaches, historic forts, delicious seafood, and world-famous Alphonso mangoes, Konkan is truly a paradise for travelers.

WHY KOKAN ?

     As the season of Ganesh Chaturthi arrives, Konkan comes alive with devotion, vibrant decorations, joyous aartis, and the sweet aroma of modaks — offering visitors a true cultural and festive experience. Alongside the celebrations, the region’s scenic beaches, historic forts, and lush greenery make Konkan an unforgettable destination during this season.
